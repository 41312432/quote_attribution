User Comments for Boogie Nights

Shaun JN (10 out of 10 )
This is one of the best movies I've ever seen. Basically, everything about it is perfect. The lighting is beautiful, the acting is superb (It's got Mark Wahlberg...what more could you want), the dialogue is witty, and the soundtrack is great. There aren't many movies like this, where you can watch it over and over again and follow it in so many different ways. Good stuff.

Max Manfre (10 out of 10 )
I love Boogie Nights a lot. Everything was great: the characters, the cars, the fashion trends. The women were very beautiful. What I like best about the film is there are a variety of characters. There were more characters then I can keep track of. Even though the main focus was on the Dirk Diggler character, all of the other stars get to steal the show for awhile.
