George (10 out of 10 )
Amazing movie. Very true to the "Bourne" style of movie. It's smart, and witty, and it is mostly made for an intelligent audience. It pulls no punches and delivers a very well constructed ending to the Bourne series. It has a lot of emotional power, while at the same time being fast-paced and keeps the viewer glued the whole way.
