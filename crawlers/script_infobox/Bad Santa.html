Matt Lohr (9 out of 10 )
Nice writing, this. Though the completed film fleshes out Willie as a character much more thoroughly, as well as making his relationship with the kid even more moving, the core of the story is already here, as well as many of the film's funniest jokes and some great descriptive writing (the kid described as "probably smelling like pee" made me laugh out loud). I also loved Willie, when he's attacking the plastic burro in the Santa display, calling it "spic!" If that's in the movie, I've missed it, but that's just great. This is a favorite screenplay of mine, and it was fun to get to read it.
