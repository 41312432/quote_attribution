Ok for those still wondering about Liar's Dice I will attempt to explain. What they are playing in the movies is Liar's Dice Common Hand. The idea is that players bid on the total of a specific face (number) on the table, not just their own dice, hence the four 4's. On your turn you can either raise the bid or call someone a liar. To raise the bid you must either increase the total of the face, e. G five 4's or increase the face e. G two 5's (you can't decrease on either so from this point you could not say one 5 or seven 4's) If a player is challenged (called a liar) all the dice are revealled if the player has less than or equal his/her bid the challenge is defeated however if the bid is more than appear on the table the Liar call is upheld . Hopefully I have explained this clearly.
