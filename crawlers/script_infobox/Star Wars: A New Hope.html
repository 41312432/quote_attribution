Toucan Sam (10 out of 10 )
As amazing as this script is, I can see why executives thought it would be a total flop. I tried to read the script with the same mindset someone from 1976 would have. Nothing like this had ever been made, and science fiction at the time was generally regarded as a genre inhabited by B-movies, with 2001: A Space Odyssey being the only real exception. If this movie had not been made as well as it was, it could have turned out really cheesy, with laughable special effects and horrible looking rubber suits for aliens. The fact it turned out the way it did shows what a genius George Lucas is, or was. It can be argued that he succeeded with the first trilogy despite himself, considering how mediocre the prequels turned out.
