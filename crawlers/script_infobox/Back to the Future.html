This was one of the best films ever made, IMHO. The overall concept was very original for its time. There had been plenty of other movies involving time travel before it, but I'd have to say that this was the first movie I've ever seen that made something so implausible (as bending the space-time continuum) seem very likely. Other movies, you see weird contraptions and spaceships doing stuff like that; but not this film--in this film the time machine is a sports car; something you can go down to the dealership and purchase in real life (note: DeLoreans really can't be purchased off any lot anymore due to their rarity, but you get the idea), therefore adding to the notion that you probably could build a time machine out of any fast vehicle you may be lucky enough to own (lemme know if it actually works, lol). As for making any more movies, nothing would ever beat the original, period. But with the recent Hollywood trend of recycling classic films (i.e. remakes), it may be a matter of time (pun unavoidable) before someone gets the idea to remake the trilogy and update it for current audiences.
