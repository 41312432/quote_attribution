Every little exclamatory utterance that Goodman and Bridges say on-screen is written down for them. Absolutely genius. The more I watch the movie, I realize how absolutely inane the whole thing is and how the story just kind of ends without any sort of finality. In my life I have been lucky enough to have a phone conversation with the real "Dude," and I am going to try to turn my birthday into a Lebowski-fest where I take part in the BIG LEBOWSKI CHALLENGE: Every time he drinks a white russian, every time he smokes one of his roaches DO AS THE DUDE DOES.
