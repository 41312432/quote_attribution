Craig Vincent (10 out of 10 )
I had the pleasure and the honor of playing the 'COWBOY' in this film and being directed by the most incredibly talented director I believe working in cinema today, but unfortunately, this script version is not the Original script that Martin Scorsese and Nicholas Pileggi wrote. It's a play by play (verbatim) version of the final cut and answer print of the film everyone has had the pleasure of seeing, but it's NOT the original. I'm not sure where the "original" screenplay might be viewed, but for true historians and film buffs alike, if you believe that what you read here in this script version was what was originally written, then I must tell you- it isn't. I personally improvised a few of my lines and was given the freedom as an actor to embellish upon what was put in front of me, and I must tell you, as an actor, given that creative lattitude by a director and actors such as Robert DeNiro and Joe Pesci was as close as an out of body experience that one could imagine. That creative safety net that those artists afforded me was a once in a lifetime experience that I know I will more than likely never be able to achieve ever again. It's the class and dignity (not to mention the talent)for which Mr. Scorsese and Mr. Deniro embody that has the creative world knocking down their doors for the opportunity just to breathe the same air. I was one of the fortunate ones, and because of the Grace of God and my Angel of a Mother, I was able to fulfil a dream and work with the best of the best in my chosen profession. May God Bless all of you and may you have at least one opportunity in life (like I had) to shine in your profession of choice. All the best, Craig Vincent.
