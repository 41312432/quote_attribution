The Mouse Avenger (10 out of 10 )
As the first person to comment on this movie, I have a lot to say... First of all, this movie is one of my yop favorites. Like my other fave flicks (i.e., The Great Mouse Detective), this is one that easily makes me feel good inside. A perfect movie to watch when you're feeling down & low. Secondly, I don't care what other people say. I believe that Prince is just as great an actor as Morris Day. 'Nuff said. Third, the music for the film is just as excellent as the movie itself. If only we had a separate soundtrack for the instrumental score...that would be sweet, too. Fourth, this movie is also special to me considering the fact that it was my first R-rated movie. "PURPLE RAIN" RULES!!!!!!
