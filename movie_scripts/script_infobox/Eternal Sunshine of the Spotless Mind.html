Ok, here is me, going against the grain. I love the movie so much, it is my favorite movie and it only goes to show my true love for it that I read the script. One thing that bothered me after reading the script was why it wasn't shown as it was. I mean, yes, the movie was another happy ending and the actors were brilliant and the characters were moving but I love this version. I love its raw edges, I love the truth of the story with Mary and the way that Joel and Clementine reacted to their tapes. It was truth and vision, in the movie the moral was "love will find a way" and the script was "Those who cannot remember the past are condemned to repeat it". There is so much difference there. I also love how the script shows both sides of Mary's point of view, on one hand people need to know what goes on with their life, on the other there are some horrific things happening in the world and maybe its better if we just can forget. My take on the movie was, that no one should have the ability to edit their lives and it shows just how ignorant people are to choose to forget a lesson learned. This is a good movie, a better script though.
