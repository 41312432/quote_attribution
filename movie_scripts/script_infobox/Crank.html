Daniel M (4 out of 10 )
If you've seen DOA (Dead on Arrival) then you will be disappointed with this script. If you haven't then you will find this a unique concept. Unfortunately, it's not, and the shoot em up style of this script really strips away any seed of creativity or originality it could have contained. Jason Statham is no Dennis Quaid. Still this is such an extradinary concept that with a director such as John Woo would have been an experience of over-the-top entertainment that would have become a cult classic. The studio responsible for "Crank" should have turned and developed this concept as high grade summer entertainment in the multiplexes, instead of giving us a straight to dvd entertainment.
