Funny story, I saw this movie in theaters with three good friends of mine, I had recently gotten a gift-card for the movie theater and I asked everybody if they wanted to go see a movie. We all decided "RockNRolla" looked like the best movie they were showing and soon we were in the theater, watching it. Two and a half hours later, we all walked out saying "WTF?" But in the end, we had a pretty good time, even if the movie was pretty horrible.

MrLJ (4 out of 10 )
You know, I read a lot of scripts, as I'm trying to perfect my latest one (I write-on my third right now-havent sold one yet), but this script, I must say, is not good. For a start, it uses 'We see', which is something that gets a script rejected, if written by a non-filmmaker; so why should these morons get away with it. And he uses it too much; just write what is happening, not what we're meant to see. Anyway, apart from that, the dialogue is good, but there isn't enough scene descriptions; it's a bit all over the place. The film was good however.
