afman (9 out of 10 )
I can't believe the bad reviews on this movie. It is a complete story about the "origin" of an earthbound hero and it's done amazingly. If you want a normal superhero movie go sit half unconsciously through fantastic four and never think about the movie again. On the other hand this is a complete and powerful film that if you have a comic book bone in your body should be easy to connect with. The beauty in this twist isn't the shocking spookiness that the sixth sense had it's the wonderful inevitability of it. This movie had to end this way to be so great but there's still no way you see it coming. Overall I give this movie 8 thumbs up.
